# APP 1.7 Automated Decision-Making Disclosure Tool — Prototype

A working prototype exploring what it takes for an Australian government agency
to determine, disclose, and prove compliance with APP 1.7 (Automated
Decision-Making), effective December 2026.

**Live demo:** [add your Netlify URL here once deployed]

## What this is

One assessment generates three outputs:
- A register entry, aligned to the DTA's Standard for Accountability
- A plain-language citizen-facing disclosure notice
- An executive summary row

The design centres on a "Documented vs Proven in Practice" distinction —
a use case can be fully written up on paper and still fail to produce a
real, case-specific explanation when someone actually asks for one.

## What this is not

- Not connected to any real agency register, CMDB, or DTA reporting system
- Not a claim to solve cross-agency aggregation — that gap is named, not fixed
- Not a finished or authoritative system — a fast prototype meant to surface
  the right questions, built to be worked through *with* the people it
  affects, not developed for them in isolation

## Stack

Single-file HTML/React prototype, no build step. Built with Claude (Fable 5)
as part of a design and governance case study.
